KANBAN PROCESS

1. Four Kanban Bins with Lock system at a defined location for Kanban Cards
with Title for dropping cards safetly.

i) KANBAN BHL Bin
ii) KANBAN SSL and; Mini bin
iii) KANBAN Scissor Bin
iv) KANBAN Boom and; TH Bin

2.Kanban identification sticker (approved by JCB) would be pasted on each and
every bin/box at Supplier's end.

3.Kanban Post (The Kanban board) to be placed in receiving area, where all the
triggered part cards will be kept as per the Supplier.

4.The receiving team (Kanban Champions) both for each shift (A and B) would
collect KANBAN card from the Kanban post and attach the card on the respective
material/part and cross-check if all material/ part is coming with the Kanban
identification Sticker.

5.It would be the duty of the Kanban Champion to collect the cards from the
respective “KANBAN Bin” and hand them over to the DO in-charge (DO
assigned for Kanban triggering).

6.It would be the duty of the Kanban DO (assigned) to raise trigger as per the
Kanban card he receives from the Kanban champions and hands the Kanban card
back to champion to be kept it back to the Kanban Post.

7.The Put away team would check if Kanban card is attached on the Bin or Box
received from Supplier before putting the item at its dedicated location.

8.Picking Team would remove the card and; safely put in respective line “Kanban
Bin” .
